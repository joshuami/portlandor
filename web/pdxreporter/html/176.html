To report a sidewalk trip hazard, and to assure a response please provide: Your name, a daytime phone number* and an actual location of the problem, for example: an address or business name. If there is not enough information in this report, the Sidewalk Maintenance office will not investigate.

Please call 503-823-1711 if you are unable to use this app to make a report. Report vegetation issues using the Sidewalk Vegetation category.

* If your user profile already includes your daytime phone number, you do not need to provide it in your report.
