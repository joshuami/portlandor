To report vegetation impeding passage on a sidewalk and to assure a response to you report, we must have:

  • Your name, address and phone number*
  • Specific address where the vegetation is located
  • Descriptive information of vegetation
  • If the vegetation is from a tree, located in the public right of way, please contact Urban Forestry at 503-823-TREE (8733)

Your information provided is confidential and we are unable to act on anonymous reports.

* If your user profile already includes your address and phone number, you do not need to provide this information in your report.
