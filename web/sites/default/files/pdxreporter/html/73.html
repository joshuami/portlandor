If you would like to have a quicker response to your reports of a vehicle that appears to be abandoned on the public right-of- way, report directly to the PBOT report form at: https://www.portlandoregon.gov/transportation/71693.

Reporting in PDXReporter will result in a slower response to your report, but if you do still want to report here, you MUST provide the following information in order for the City to investigate your report.

REQUIRED INFORMATION:

  • Your name and daytime phone number*
  • Address where the vehicle is parked
  • Make, color and body style
  • License plate number and state, if available
  • Description of vehicle, i.e. missing wheels or parts

* If your user profile already includes your daytime phone number, you do not need to provide it in your report.

Website: Portland Bureau of Transportation
