To report a sidewalk café in violation of Chapter 17.25 of the Portland City Code, such as insufficient pedestrian clearance or low-hanging umbrellas, please provide the following information:

  • Your name and a daytime phone number*
  • Name and address of the business in violation
  • A photograph of the violation
  • A description of the violation

* If your user profile already includes your daytime phone number, you do not need to provide it in your report.
